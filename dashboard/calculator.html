How earnings work: Relay operators earn sats from every paid service call routed through their node. AI inference, storage writes, and identity operations all generate revenue at the published rate card. Free-tier calls (within monthly quotas) don't generate revenue, but attract apps that eventually exceed free limits. Revenue is settled via Lightning Network. Operators keep 100% of service fees on their own node.

Assumptions: Default rates from the HiveRelay pricing engine. ~40% of input tokens, ~60% output tokens per AI call. Storage writes average 4KB. All estimates assume consistent daily traffic. Actual earnings depend on app adoption, network effects, and your relay's uptime and reputation score.

Getting started: Run npx hiverelay start --mode public on any VPS or home server. All services are free by default. Operators earn 1,000 welcome credits on startup. Apps earn 1,000 credits on first connect.
